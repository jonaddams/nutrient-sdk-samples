# Non-Disclosure Agreement

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

---

## Parties

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **Disclosing Party:** Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation with its principal office at 100 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105 ("Acme")
- **Receiving Party:** ______________________________ ("Recipient")

## 1. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information, including but not limited to:

- Trade secrets, inventions, and patent applications
- Business plans, strategies, and financial data
- Customer lists, pricing models, and marketing plans
- Software, source code, algorithms, and technical specifications
- Any information marked as "Confidential" or "Proprietary"

## 2. Obligations of the Receiving Party

The Recipient agrees to:

1. Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
2. Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
3. Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating a potential business relationship
4. Protect Confidential Information using the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care

## 3. Exclusions

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient
- Was already known to the Recipient prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed by the Recipient without use of Confidential Information
- Is disclosed pursuant to a court order or legal requirement

## 4. Term and Termination

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **two (2) years** from the Effective Date. The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination for an additional **three (3) years**.

## 5. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **State of California**, without regard to conflict of law principles.
