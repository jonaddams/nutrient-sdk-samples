Section 4: Reading Comprehension
Read the passage below, then highlight the key facts about Jupiter.
Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, with a diameter of about 143,000 kilometers. It is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium. Jupiter’s Great Red Spot is a massive storm that has been raging for hundreds of years. The planet has at least 95 known moons, including the four large Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
